# Known Connections Reference Set for T1

**Task T1:** "What is the relationship between language, consciousness, and mathematical structure?"

## Purpose

This reference set lists cross-domain connections that are KNOWN in the literature. For M5 (Generative Novelty) scoring, any connection that appears in this set is NOT novel — even if well-expressed. Novelty requires going beyond these.

---

## Known Connections (Not Novel)

### Language ↔ Consciousness
1. **Sapir-Whorf hypothesis** — language shapes/constrains conscious experience (linguistic relativity)
2. **Inner speech and consciousness** — Vygotsky's internalization, Carruthers' inner speech theory
3. **Higher-order thought theories** — language as medium for higher-order representation of mental states
4. **Global workspace theory + language** — language as a "broadcasting" medium in Baars' global workspace
5. **Wittgenstein's Tractatus** — limits of language = limits of the world; the mystical as what cannot be spoken
6. **Phenomenological approaches** — Husserl on the relationship between expression and Erlebnis; Merleau-Ponty on pre-linguistic meaning
7. **Neural correlates** — Language areas (Broca's, Wernicke's) implicated in conscious cognitive processing

### Language ↔ Mathematical Structure
8. **Chomsky hierarchy** — formal languages classified by computational/mathematical complexity
9. **Montague grammar** — natural language semantics formalized in type theory and lambda calculus
10. **Categorical grammar** — language as a type-logical system (Lambek calculus, pregroup grammars)
11. **Information-theoretic measures of language** — entropy rate, surprisal, mutual information in language processing
12. **Distributional semantics** — vector space models, word2vec; meaning as geometry
13. **Topos-theoretic semantics** — Abramsky, Coecke: meaning in categorical quantum mechanics applied to language (DisCoCat)

### Consciousness ↔ Mathematical Structure
14. **Integrated Information Theory (IIT)** — Tononi's Φ as a mathematical measure of consciousness
15. **Penrose-Hameroff Orch-OR** — consciousness arising from quantum gravity effects in microtubules
16. **Quantum consciousness models** — Stapp, Hameroff; quantum mechanics as substrate for consciousness
17. **Mathematical Platonism + consciousness** — Penrose: mathematical reality is accessed by consciousness (Gödel argument)
18. **Gödel's incompleteness and minds** — Lucas/Penrose argument that minds are not Turing machines because they can see Gödelian truths
19. **Category-theoretic models of consciousness** — consciousness as a functor or natural transformation in some categorical framework (Tsuchiya, Taguchi)

### Three-Way Connections (Language ↔ Consciousness ↔ Math)
20. **Formal models of intentionality** — mathematical models of "aboutness" connecting all three
21. **Wittgenstein's philosophy** — the picture theory (Tractatus) as a three-way bridge
22. **Embodied mathematics (Lakoff & Núñez)** — mathematical concepts grounded in bodily experience, expressed through language
23. **AI and the Chinese Room** — Searle's argument connecting syntax (math), semantics (meaning), and consciousness
24. **Universal grammar as mathematical structure** — generative grammar as both mathematical and connected to innate cognitive (conscious?) capacity
25. **Quantum linguistics** — applying quantum formalism to model meaning in context; Bruza, Busemeyer, Pothos

## Scoring Guidance

- **M5 = 0–1:** Output contains ONLY connections from this list, possibly rephrased
- **M5 = 2–3:** Output contains 1–3 connections NOT on this list that are genuinely interesting
