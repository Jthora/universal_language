# Domain List for T1

**Task T1:** "What is the relationship between language, consciousness, and mathematical structure?"

## Pre-Specified Domains for M1 Scoring

A domain counts toward M1 if the output makes at least one substantive claim, defines a concept, or draws a relationship *within* that domain using domain-specific technical vocabulary.

| # | Domain | Examples of Substantive Engagement | Examples That Do NOT Count |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Formal linguistics** | Chomsky hierarchy, generative grammar, syntax trees, morphology, phonological rules, recursion in language | "Language has rules" |
| 2 | **Cognitive science / neuroscience** | Neural correlates of consciousness, binding problem, global workspace theory, integrated information theory (IIT), embodied cognition | "The brain processes language" |
| 3 | **Quantum mechanics** | Wave functions, superposition, measurement problem, Hilbert spaces, operators, decoherence, entanglement | "Quantum physics is weird" |
| 4 | **Philosophy of mind** | Hard problem of consciousness, qualia, functionalism, panpsychism, intentionality, phenomenology, Nagel's "what is it like" | "Consciousness is mysterious" |
| 5 | **Mathematical logic / foundations** | Gödel's incompleteness, model theory, type theory, proof theory, computability, decidability, formal semantics | "Math is logical" |
| 6 | **Information theory** | Shannon entropy, mutual information, channel capacity, Kolmogorov complexity, coding theory | "Information is important" |
| 7 | **Category theory / algebra** | Functors, natural transformations, adjunctions, monoids, groups, algebras, universal properties | No count unless category-theoretic concepts are actually used |
| 8 | **Semiotics / philosophy of language** | Signs, signifiers, reference, Wittgenstein's language games, speech acts, pragmatics, meaning as use | "Language conveys meaning" |
| 9 | **Topology / geometry** | Manifolds, fiber bundles, curvature, metric spaces, homotopy, Erlangen program | No count unless geometric concepts are actually invoked |
| 10 | **Quantum cognition / quantum linguistics** | Quantum probability in decision-making, quantum semantics, interaction-free measurements as cognitive models | Merely saying "quantum and cognition are related" |

**Maximum M1 score for T1: 10**

**Scoring note:** Domains NOT on this list can still be counted if the evaluator judges them to be genuinely distinct from the above and substantively engaged. If a new domain is counted, note it and its justification. Maximum 2 bonus domains beyond the list.
