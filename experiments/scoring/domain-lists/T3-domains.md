# Domain List for T3

**Task T3:** "Design a formal framework for understanding how metaphor creates meaning."

## Pre-Specified Domains for M1 Scoring

| # | Domain | Examples of Substantive Engagement | Examples That Do NOT Count |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Cognitive linguistics** | Lakoff & Johnson's conceptual metaphor theory, source/target domains, image schemas, blending theory, Fauconnier & Turner | "Metaphors compare two things" |
| 2 | **Formal semantics** | Lambda calculus for meaning, compositional semantics, type-theoretic semantics, Montague grammar, possible worlds | "Semantics is about meaning" |
| 3 | **Category theory** | Functors as structure-preserving maps (metaphor as functor!), natural transformations, adjunctions, Yoneda lemma | No count unless category-theoretic concepts are used in the framework |
| 4 | **Topology / geometry** | Semantic spaces as manifolds, metaphor as continuous deformation, geodesics in meaning space, curvature of conceptual regions | "Meanings are close or far apart" |
| 5 | **Computational linguistics / NLP** | Word embeddings, distributional semantics, vector space models, attention mechanisms, transformer representations | "Computers can process language" |
| 6 | **Philosophy of language** | Reference, sense vs. reference (Frege), speech acts (Austin/Searle), Wittgenstein, pragmatics, Grice's maxims | "Language is used to communicate" |
| 7 | **Rhetoric / literary theory** | Figures of speech taxonomy, tenor/vehicle, allegory, synecdoche, metonymy as distinct from metaphor | "Metaphors are literary devices" |
| 8 | **Neuroscience of language** | Neural substrate of metaphor processing, embodied simulation, mirror neurons, right-hemisphere involvement, N400 ERP | "The brain processes metaphors" |
| 9 | **Mathematics (other)** | Group actions (metaphor as group action on meaning), set-theoretic models, lattice theory, order theory | Only if mathematical structures are used in framework design |
| 10 | **Information theory** | Metaphor as lossy compression, mutual information between domains, channel capacity constraints on metaphorical mapping | "Metaphors simplify complex ideas" |

**Maximum M1 score for T3: 10**
